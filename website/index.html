§ CIRC.03 — Circulation Procedure

From install to first checkout.

Four steps from first launch to a working circulation desk, shared across every workstation on your network.

01

Install & connect to MySQL

Download the installer for your OS — Java is bundled, nothing else to install. Point the app at your MySQL 8 instance; Flyway runs the schema automatically on first launch.

02

Catalogue your collection

Add books with full metadata, assign copies, and print QR or barcode labels straight from the desk. Bulk import handles large catalogues in one pass.

03

Register members & assign roles

Enrol students and faculty individually or import from a CSV. Assign each user a role; permissions follow automatically — no manual gate-keeping required.

04

Issue, track, and close loans

Scan a barcode, select the borrower, confirm. The system tracks due dates, accumulates fines, manages reserves, and generates a PDF receipt on return.
