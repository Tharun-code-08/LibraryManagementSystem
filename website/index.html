What's on the shelves

14 modules, one desk
CAT.02

Catalog & Copies

Authors, publishers, nested categories, and per-copy tracking — with QR codes and Code128 barcode labels generated on save.

PPL.03

Students & Faculty

Registration, membership types, and bulk import from CSV or Excel, each with a per-row rejected-rows report.

CIRC.14

Issue, Return, Reserve

Barcode-scan-driven circulation desk, FIFO reservation queueing, borrow-limit and grace-period enforcement.

FIN.05

Fines & Payments

Overdue and condition-based fines, partial payments, waivers, and one-page PDF receipts.

INV.06

Procurement & Audits

Supplier and purchase-order workflow through approval to receipt, plus shelf-verification inventory audits.

RPT.08

Dashboard & Reports

Live stat cards and trend charts, ten exportable report types to PDF or Excel.

SEC.09

Roles & Permissions

Every state-changing action is gated by a permission code — librarian, student, faculty, and admin see exactly what they're allowed to touch.

ADM.10

Admin & Backup

User and role management, a full audit trail, system settings, and one-click database backup/restore.

Take it to your desk

Each installer bundles its own Java runtime — nothing else to install. Point it at your library's shared MySQL database on first run and Flyway sets up the schema automatically.
